About sfghb

Searching for God's Heartbeat (SFGHB) seeks to spread the gospel of Christ Jesus through out Daily Devotional. Like Jesus commanded, we keep sending the undiluted word of God. It is a brainchild of the Sister's Fellowship International, a non-profit, non-denominational Christian Fellowship.
